Privacy Policy

Protecting Your Trust & Information at LPN Lanka Nexus

LPN Lanka Nexus Real Estate Construction (“Company”, “we”, “our”, “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when visiting our website or interacting with our services.

1 Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from you when you:

  • Fill out contact forms or property inquiry forms
  • Subscribe to updates or newsletters
  • Communicate with us via phone, email, or WhatsApp
  • Make payments or request quotations

The information collected may include:

  • Full Name, Phone Number, and Email Address
  • Property preferences and Location details
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide

2 How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for purposes related to our services:

  • Responding to property inquiries and requests
  • Providing information about properties, projects, or services
  • Customer support and communication
  • Legal documentation and contract processing
  • Improving our website and services
  • Marketing communications, only if you have given consent

3 Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Information may only be shared in the following cases:

  • With trusted service providers working on our behalf
  • If required by law or government authorities
  • To protect the legal rights or property of the company

4 Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or theft. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee 100% security.

5 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience, analyze website traffic, and remember user preferences. You can choose to disable cookies via your browser settings.

6 Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external websites.

7 Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the details below.

8 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All updates will be posted on this page with the revised date.
